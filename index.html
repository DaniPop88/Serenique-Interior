About Serenique Interior Haven
Serenique Interior Haven began with a simple belief: good design should be warm, functional, and made to last. Founded and run by Anonuevo (Serena Marie Asistio), we serve homeowners and small businesses across Cavite and nearby provinces with bespoke furnishings, décor, and interior styling services.
Our Story
Serena Marie started Serenique after years of sourcing local materials and working with carpenters and textile artisans. What started as custom pillows and small furniture orders quickly grew into a full interior furnishings studio.
Our Mission
To make elegant, practical interiors accessible to Filipino families — blending local craftsmanship with modern design.
Our Values
Local craft first • Honest pricing • Customer care • Sustainability

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept custom orders?
Yes — custom pieces are our specialty. 30% deposit required.
Can you match a fabric or finish I like?
Absolutely. Bring a sample or photo and we’ll offer similar options from our suppliers.
How long does delivery & installation take?
Stock items: 3–10 working days. Custom: 2–8 weeks depending on complexity.
Payment methods?
We accept cash, bank transfer, and GCash/PayMaya.
Do you provide after-sales service or warranty?
We offer a 6-month workmanship warranty on custom furniture (terms apply).
Do you ship outside Cavite?
Yes — we can arrange interprovincial delivery for an additional charge.
